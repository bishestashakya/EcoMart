Research Overview


Before we started designing the EcoMart website, our team researched modern web design trends, especially for eco-friendly and online shopping websites. We explored different websites to understand how they are organized, how users navigate them, how blogs are presented, and what features online stores usually have. Some sites were used as direct references, while others were reviewed just for inspiration.


Website References Considered


1. Awwwards


We visited Awwwards to see modern and award-winning website designs. Most of the sites were very complex and had many animations, so we didn’t use any direct design elements from them. However, it helped us understand current design trends.


2. Locktree (Eco-friendly website)


We used Locktree as a reference for the blog page layout. The clean and eco-friendly presentation of Locktree inspired our blog section in EcoMart.


3. Daraz, Amazon, and eBay


We reviewed these major e-commerce platforms to understand common shopping features like:


* Add to Cart

* Product display format

* Homepage product sliders


4. Pinterest


Pinterest gave us visual inspiration. A specific screenshot from Pinterest became the main reference for EcoMart’s layout, spacing, and typography.

Similarities Identified


* We followed a clean and simple layout inspired by Pinterest


* The eco-friendly theme is similar to Lochtree


* Product cards and sliders are influenced by e-commerce platforms


* Navigation is kept simple for better user experience


Differences Identified


* EcoMart avoids heavy animations and complex UI


* The website is designed for beginner-level technical skills


* The focus is on simplicity and clarity


* Features are basic and project-based, not advanced


Improvements Implemented in EcoMart


* Simple layout for better readability


* Clean blog structure inspired by Lochtree


* Basic e-commerce features influenced by major platforms


* Eco-friendly colors and typography


* Clear buttons and navigation
